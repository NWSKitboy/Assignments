Biography

Matthew Mercer is an American voice actor and content creator. He is best known for being a dungeon master for the live dungeons and dragons show called "Critical Role". This show is comprised of him and his voice actor friends playing D and D while streaming live for an audience. The show became so popular it has even been adapted into an animated series on amazon prime called "The Legend of Vox Machina"
